ABOUT WARM HEARTS

Warm Hearts is a community initiative dedicated to ensuring that children in need have access to warm winter coats, helping them stay healthy and attend school regularly. By collecting both gently used and new coats, Warm Hearts supports families who struggle to provide adequate winter clothing for their children. This effort not only addresses immediate needs but also fosters a caring community that looks out for its most vulnerable members.

OUR VISION AND OUR GOALS

OUR VISION

Warm Hearts envisions a community where every child has the necessary clothing to stay warm and healthy during the cold winter months.

OUR GOALS

We aim to gather around 1,000 coats each winter season to distribute to children in need. By offering free winter coats, we reduce the financial burden on families, ensuring no child must face the cold unprotected. Our efforts promote community involvement, foster support among residents, and encourage everyone to care for our children.

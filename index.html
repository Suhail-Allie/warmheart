OUR MISSION

Warm Hearts helps families facing winter clothing shortages by providing gently used and unused coats to local schools and community centers. By doing so, we reduce health issues, decrease school absences, and alleviate the discomfort of children during the cold winter months. Our initiative ensures that coats reach those who need them the most, making a tangible difference in the lives of children and their families.

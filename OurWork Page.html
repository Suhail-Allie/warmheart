HOW WARM HEARTS WORK

Coat Collection

Donation Bins: We place donation bins in easily accessible locations such as schools, libraries, and community centers to make it easy for people to contribute.

Coat Drive Events: We organize events where community members can donate coats and more, creating a sense of unity and purpose around this cause.

Sorting and Cleaning

Volunteer Support: Our dedicated volunteers sort the donated coats by size and condition, ensuring that each coat is suitable for distribution.

Cleaning: Coats that require cleaning are washed to ensure they are ready for use, maintaining the sanitation and comfort of the recipients.

Distribution

Collaborative Effort: We work closely with local schools, shelters, and community organizations to distribute coats directly to children in need.

Targeted Reach: By partnering with these organizations, we ensure that the coats are delivered to those who need them the most, maximizing our impact.
